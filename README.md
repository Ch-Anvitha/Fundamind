# Fundamind 💰
### Learn Money

A financial literacy gaming platform that transforms complex money concepts into engaging, interactive experiences — built for the 67% of Indians who lack basic financial knowledge.

---

## 💡 The Problem
67% of Indians are financially illiterate — no basic knowledge of saving, investing, or financial planning. Existing solutions are either too boring (textbooks) or too complex (financial apps).

## 🚀 Our Solution
Fundamind is India's first gamified financial education platform — think Duolingo, but for money. Designed for everyone from curious 12-year-olds to CA aspirants.

## ✨ Features
- **Game Modules** — Budget Bazaar, Stock Market Simulator, Retirement Racer (3 difficulty levels)
- **FinShorts** — 30–60 second vertical videos explaining money concepts in plain language
- **CA Syllabus Integration** — First platform to gamify the CA Foundation + Intermediate curriculum
- **Gamification** — Coins, badges, streaks, leaderboards, and progress dashboards
- **Multi-language** — English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi
- **Offline Mode** — Works without internet, built for rural India
- **B2B Dashboard** — Teacher and parent monitoring tools
